The story behind AniCollect - Where passion meets collectibles

Our Story

Founded in 2024, AniCollect was born from a simple yet powerful mission: to provide anime fans worldwide with premium quality collectibles and fashion that truly honor the series they love.

We're not just sellers - we're fellow otaku who understand the importance of authenticity, quality, and attention to detail. Every product in our collection is carefully selected and verified to ensure it meets our high standards.

From limited edition figures to exclusive fashion pieces, we partner directly with manufacturers and artists to bring you items that are worth every yen.

Our Values

Every item is verified authentic. No bootlegs, no fakes - only genuine products from licensed manufacturers.

Premium Quality

Community First

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all your products authentic?

+

Absolutely! We work directly with licensed manufacturers and distributors. Every product comes with a certificate of authenticity. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit items.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes! We ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Express shipping options are available for urgent orders.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened items in original packaging. If an item arrives damaged, we'll replace it immediately at no cost to you. Contact our support team within 7 days of receiving your order.

How do I know when new products are available?

+

Subscribe to our newsletter for early access notifications! We also announce new arrivals on our social media channels. Premium members get 24-hour early access to limited releases.

Do you accept pre-orders?

+

Yes! We accept pre-orders for upcoming releases. A deposit is required to secure your pre-order, with the balance due before shipping. You'll receive regular updates on your pre-order status.

Can I request specific products?

+

We love hearing from our community! Use our contact form to request specific products. While we can't guarantee we'll stock everything, customer requests heavily influence our sourcing decisions.
