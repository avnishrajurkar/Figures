FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does shipping take?

Domestic shipping within Japan takes 2-4 business days. International shipping varies by location: USA/Canada (7-14 days), Europe (10-18 days), Asia (5-10 days). Express shipping options are available at checkout.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely! We source all our products directly from licensed manufacturers and distributors. Every item comes with a certificate of authenticity. We guarantee 100% genuine products or your money back.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened items in original condition. Pre-order items can be cancelled before shipment. Damaged or defective items are eligible for immediate replacement or full refund including shipping costs.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight. Free international shipping on orders over $150 USD.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also log into your account to view order status and tracking information. For pre-orders, we'll notify you when the item is ready to ship.
