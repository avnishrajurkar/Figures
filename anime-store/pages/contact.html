Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?
We offer multiple shipping options including standard (5-7 business days), express (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping. International shipping is available to most countries with tracking included.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's site to track your package in real-time.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Pre-order items can be cancelled before shipment. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
Are your products authentic?
Yes! All our figures and merchandise are 100% authentic and sourced directly from manufacturers and licensed distributors. We guarantee authenticity on every product.
Do you offer pre-orders?
Absolutely! We accept pre-orders for upcoming releases. A deposit may be required, and you'll be notified when your item is ready to ship.
