Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. International shipping varies by location but typically takes 10-21 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

Are your products authentic? +

Absolutely! We work directly with licensed manufacturers and distributors to ensure all our products are 100% authentic. Each item comes with a certificate of authenticity.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Pre-owned or damaged items cannot be returned. Return shipping costs are covered by the customer unless the item was defective.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes! We ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees and import duties may apply depending on your country.

Can I pre-order upcoming items? +

Yes! We offer pre-orders for many upcoming releases. Pre-order items will be shipped as soon as they become available. You'll be notified when your order ships.
